What is the grid ?

The grid is the "real world", the "adult life".
You've lived on the grid your whole life.
But haven't participated in any of it's rewarding activites.
Wether you like it, repress it or deal with it, we all take part in it at one point or another.

What is Grid ?

Grid is a webpage that compiles and simplifies information that is vital to your day-to-day life.
We take "real life" concepts, processes and terms, and turn them into understandable, but most importantly, useful pieces of information.

Why Grid ?

Our daily struggles are usually propelled by a constant lack of information.
Be it because school never covered it, it was too complicated or your parents have a "throw 'em to the wolves" perspective, we face situations where we're expected to know things that we don't.
We strive to make information accesible, understandable and useful .
